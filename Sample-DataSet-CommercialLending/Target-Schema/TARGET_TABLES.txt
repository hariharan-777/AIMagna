
# Target (BigQuery) Tables: Schemas & Descriptions

## Dimensions
- **dim_date**: Calendar attributes for reporting keyed by `date_key`.
- **dim_borrower**: Borrower master attributes.
- **dim_rate_index**: Rate index master attributes.
- **dim_facility**: Facility attributes.
- **dim_loan**: Loan master attributes enriched with rate index details.
- **dim_collateral**: Collateral master attributes.
- **dim_guarantor**: Guarantor master attributes.
- **dim_syndicate_member**: Syndicate bank master.
- **dim_risk_rating**: Latest rating per loan for slicing facts by credit quality.

## Facts
- **fact_payments**: Fact table of payment events with measures and linkage to loan and borrower.
- **fact_loan_snapshot**: Point-in-time loan balances and risk metrics captured at last payment date.
