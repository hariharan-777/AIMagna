
# Source Tables: Schemas & Descriptions

- **borrower**: Legal entity responsible for loans; master data including industry and address.
- **rate_index**: Floating rate index master with tenor and currency.
- **rate_index_history**: Historical daily/periodic rates linked to rate_index.
- **facility**: Credit facility under which loans are originated.
- **loan**: Individual loans including pricing and schedule metadata.
- **collateral**: Collateral items linked to loans and valued over time.
- **guarantor**: Guarantors linked to borrowers.
- **covenant**: Financial covenants linked to loans.
- **syndicate_member**: Banks in syndicates.
- **syndicate_participation**: Link table between loans and syndicate members including commitments.
- **risk_rating**: Historical and current risk ratings for loans and borrowers.
- **payment**: Cash payments collected for loans, broken down by components.
